# Evidence Room

This folder stores proof artifacts used by the evidence-to-scale layer.

Important: the included files are examples and templates. They are marked as `example_placeholder` and do not count as external market evidence. Replace them with dated, source-verifiable records when real evidence exists.

Recommended folders:

- `market/` — customer discovery and design partner records.
- `pilots/` — pilot outcome dockets.
- `benchmarks/` — baseline and replay results.
- `commercial/` — private commercial evidence records.
- `validation/` — independent validator or reviewer records.
- `fair-value/` — formal fair-value-support records.
- `proof-velocity/events/` — mission-to-proof timing events.
- `strategic-scale/` — master dockets for strategic evidence review.

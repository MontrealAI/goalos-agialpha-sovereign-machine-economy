# Brand Identity

## Tone

Institutional, precise, elegant, ambitious, evidence-first, calm under scrutiny.

## Core line

```text
AI creates output. GoalOS creates proof.
```

## Public framing

```text
GoalOS turns autonomous AI work into evidence-bearing institutional capability.
```

## Visual direction

- Deep institutional dark background.
- Gold, violet, and blue highlights.
- Large editorial typography.
- Clear cards for proof artifacts and operating laws.
- Claim boundary always visible.

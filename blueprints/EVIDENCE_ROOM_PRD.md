# Evidence Room PRD

## Product promise

A private stakeholder room that organizes proof artifacts, confidence levels, source records, validator decisions, and publication status.

## Users

- Founder or operator.
- Board reviewer.
- Enterprise buyer.
- Validator.
- Contributor.

## Core features

- Evidence artifact index.
- Status filters.
- Public/private flagging.
- Claim linkage.
- Reviewer notes.
- Evidence hashes.
- Exportable proof packet.

## Non-goals

The product does not create false traction, automate legal review, or replace formal valuation work.

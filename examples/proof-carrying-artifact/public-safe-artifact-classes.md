# Public-safe artifact examples

Use these examples in the Proof-Carrying Artifact Foundry:

1. **Repository launch readiness capability** — checks README, website pages, reports, claim boundary, and replay path.
2. **Evidence Docket reviewer workflow** — guides a reviewer through claims, baselines, proof packets, replay, and validator notes.
3. **Falsification gauntlet protocol** — tests whether strong claims survive baselines and stress conditions.
4. **Public-private proof boundary policy** — keeps private intelligence private while exposing public-safe proof commitments.
5. **Proof mission template** — transforms a public-safe objective into mission contract, claims matrix, and validator packet.

Do not enter personal data, customer data, credentials, secrets, private keys, seed phrases, payment data, confidential data, regulated data, or trade secrets.

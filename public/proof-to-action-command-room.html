What this demonstrates

GoalOS does not stop at output. It converts an objective into proof, and proof into a governed state that a human can inspect, challenge, approve, reject, or revise.

Why this matters

High-stakes AI work needs actionability, rollback conditions, risk visibility, evidence traceability, and reusable capability — not just persuasive prose.

What it does not do

It does not approve production action, move funds, connect a wallet, call a model, submit data, or replace legal, security, compliance, investment, or human review.

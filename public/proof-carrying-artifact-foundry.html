Plain English

The central object is not the agent.

An agent may help produce work, but GoalOS does not let a useful-looking output automatically become trusted institutional memory. It must become a proof-carrying artifact: versioned, scoped, evaluated, rollbackable, and reviewable.

Advanced view

Score is advisory. Gates are mandatory.

The Proof Gradient score may improve, but promotion still requires proof validity, evaluation pass, rollback readiness, scope authorization, and challenge-window clearance. High novelty raises the burden of proof.

Public-private boundary

Private intelligence stays private.

The public artifact carries hashes, attestations, selection status, and replay pointers. Private prompts, customer data, raw traces, secrets, and privileged workpapers do not belong in the public proof surface.

Claim Boundary

GoalOS does not claim production authority, investment return, legal advice, tax advice, security assurance, achieved AGI/ASI, state-of-the-art empirical performance, mainnet authorization, wallet support, custody, or live transaction capability from this public-alpha repository.

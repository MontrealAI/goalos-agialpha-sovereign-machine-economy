Human Visual QA

Proof means humans can read it.

V44.1 is the corrective pass for public-alpha readability, route integrity, and visual proof. It exists because generated UI is not acceptable until a human can inspect it without overlap, hidden text, or decorative graph clutter.

01 Open

Open the homepage, Gold Master, Autonomy Theatre, Agent Workbench, Validation Tower, Contract Atlas, All Pages, Search, and Site Health.

02 Read

Confirm every heading, body paragraph, button, and card is readable on desktop and mobile.

03 Flow

Confirm the visual proof path is a grid of cards. No line, connector, orbit, or div may hide text.

04 Boundary

Confirm no user data, no wallet, no transaction, no network call, and no production authority remain visible.

05 Verdict

Record pass/fail in the V44.1 human QA issue before publishing a corrective release.

Mission Contract

A bounded objective with success criteria, constraints, risk class, authority, and review requirements.

Proof Job

A unit of work whose output must be supported by traceable proof.

Tool Trace

A record of tool decisions, permissions, observations, and outputs.

Evidence Docket

The proof room tying claims to evidence, baselines, proof packets, costs, risks, replay, and boundaries.

Validator Report

An accept / reject / revise review of the docket and claim.

Governed Decision State

The inspectable state produced when evidence is ready for human decision.

Chronicle

Durable institutional memory of missions, proof, decisions, and capability packages.

Capability Package

A reusable workflow or artifact admitted only after evidence and review.

Human Boundary

The rule that public-alpha demos do not grant external authority or production activation.

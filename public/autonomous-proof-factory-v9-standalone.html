AGI Agent role contracts

V9 separates roles before work begins. The point is not many agents talking; the point is bounded responsibilities and traceable artifacts.

Role separation law

Planner does not validate itself. Builder does not grade itself. Sentinel can stop the route. Human authority remains required for high-impact outcomes.

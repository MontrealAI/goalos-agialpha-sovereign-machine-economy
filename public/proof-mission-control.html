Why this page matters

GoalOS already has a public demo journey. Mission Control turns that journey into operating discipline: every proposed mission gets a lane, a gate state, a reviewer path, and a docket plan. It is the bridge from impressive demos to Proof Run 001.

Plain-English rule

A mission is not ready because it sounds important. It is ready when the objective is bounded, the proof artifacts are named, replay is possible, reviewers know what to inspect, and the claim boundary is visible.

Advanced rule

Readiness is a function of Mission Contract completeness, Evidence Docket coverage, baseline presence, replayability, validator quorum, cost/risk ledger status, and public/private boundary integrity. Score is advisory. Gates are mandatory.

Claim Boundary

GoalOS does not claim production authority, investment return, legal advice, tax advice, security assurance, achieved AGI/ASI, state-of-the-art empirical performance, mainnet authorization, wallet support, custody, or live transaction capability from this public-alpha repository. No user data. No user funds. No wallet. No transaction. Human review required.

Why this matters

Blockchain-native, not crypto-first

The demo shows the ledger as compact public accountability: commitments, attestations, challenge windows, and rollback receipts. It does not expose private intelligence.

Challengeable evolution

Evolution rights do not appear because a model sounds persuasive. They appear only when proof, eval, scope, challenge, and rollback gates clear.

Institutional memory

The ledger is the memory spine for what may be challenged, rolled back, adopted, or reused later.

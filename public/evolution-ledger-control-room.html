      What this demonstrates

      The ledger is not where intelligence lives. The ledger is where commitments, hashes, attestations, challenge windows, selection decisions, and rollback receipts become durable enough for review.

      Why it matters

      Private prompts, traces, customer data, workpapers, secrets, and sensitive tool outputs stay private. Public proof stays minimal, challengeable, and review-ready.

      What blocks evolution

      Missing proof roots, missing evaluator attestations, weak validator quorum, absent rollback, open challenge windows, or a broken public/private boundary.

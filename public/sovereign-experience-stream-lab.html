Why this matters

A proof run proves what happened once. A sovereign experience stream preserves what happened across runs so future routing, reward governance, world models, temporal options, and safety policies can improve without silently learning from poisoned or unverifiable traces.

Validator ≠ reward

A validator can accept a run today. A grounded reward tracks consequences over time: cost, safety, reproducibility, delayed outcome, and usefulness. GoalOS separates acceptance from learning.

Learning is gated

The router update remains blocked until provenance, replay, reward ledger, risk ledger, quarantine, and human boundary gates pass. Score is advisory. Gates are mandatory.

Claim Boundary

GoalOS does not claim production authority, investment return, legal advice, tax advice, security assurance, achieved AGI/ASI, state-of-the-art empirical performance, mainnet authorization, wallet support, custody, or live transaction capability from this public-alpha repository.

The proof discipline

The system does not let attractive language become authority.

The V25 Evidence Docket shows the correct behavior: it supports bounded claims like “GoalOS can turn the objective into a structured proof package” and “the package is more inspectable than unstructured LLM output,” while blocking stronger claims like “the result is externally validated” and “the result guarantees ROI” until reviewer verdicts and real measurements exist.

That is the proof-gated culture.

Supported claims pass.
Weak claims get repaired.
Unsupported claims get blocked.
Missing evidence becomes AGI Jobs.

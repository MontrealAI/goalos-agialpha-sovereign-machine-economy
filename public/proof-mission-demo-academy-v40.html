Plain-language academy

From output to proof.

This page explains the end-to-end demo in normal language.

AGI Agent

Who does what?

Agents are role-bound workers: architect, planner, research, builder, verifier, node, sentinel, docket, Chronicle.

AGI Job

What is bounded?

The job defines objective, constraints, acceptance tests, risk, allowed actions, and done condition.

AGI Node

What validates?

The node prepares deterministic public-safe checks and handoff objects; high-impact decisions still require human review.

ProofBundle

What is the receipt?

It packages artifacts, hashes, cost, risk, signatures, replay path, validation result, and Chronicle pointer.

Evidence Docket

What is the proof room?

It connects claims to baselines, proof packets, risk/cost ledgers, evaluator notes, and claim boundaries.

Chronicle

What compounds?

Accepted work becomes memory and reusable capability for harder future missions.

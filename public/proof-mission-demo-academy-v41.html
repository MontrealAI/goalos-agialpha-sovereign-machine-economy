What happens
after you type?

GoalOS converts a normal objective into bounded work, proof, validation, memory, and reusable capability. This page explains every step without requiring technical background.

The one-line map

Objective → Agents → AGI Job → AGI Node → ProofBundle → Evidence Docket → Validate → Chronicle → Reusable capability.

AGI Agent

The role that frames, plans, researches, builds, verifies, packages, or records the work. Agents do not receive unlimited authority.

AGI Job

A bounded work contract with success criteria, allowed actions, risk class, proof requirements, and done condition.

AGI Node

A deterministic runtime or validation surface for public-safe checks, replay readiness, and handoff packaging.

ProofBundle

The receipt package: inputs, outputs, hashes, policies, logs, cost/risk, validator notes, and replay instructions.

Evidence Docket

The proof room. It explains what is claimed, what is not claimed, what evidence exists, what failed, and how to review.

Chronicle

Accepted work becomes institutional memory and may become reusable only after proof and review gates.

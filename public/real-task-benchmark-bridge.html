Why this matters

A public demo can show the architecture. A real-task benchmark shows whether the architecture actually improves verified work under equal constraints. This bridge teaches the upgrade path from impressive demo to empirical evidence.

A real task, strong baselines, replayable proof, cost/risk ledgers, validator review, delayed-outcome tracking, and independent reviewer access. If a baseline wins, GoalOS revises the claim.

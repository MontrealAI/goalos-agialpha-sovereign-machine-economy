Why this matters

A proof system becomes credible when an independent reviewer can inspect it without asking for private data, wallets, or trust. This page turns review into a guided, public-safe path: docket, replay, baselines, cost/risk, claim boundary, dissent, and verdict.

Plain English

A good reviewer does not ask “does this sound impressive?” They ask: can I replay it, challenge it, see what failed, and know exactly what is not claimed?

Technical note

The browser demo exports deterministic JSON/Markdown artifacts only. It does not call a model, API, wallet, storage API, analytics service, or external network endpoint.

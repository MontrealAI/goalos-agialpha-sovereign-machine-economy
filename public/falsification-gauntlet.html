Plain English

A claim is not strong because it sounds impressive. It is strong when it survives replay, baselines, safety, privacy, cost, validator review, and delayed-outcome checks.

Advanced view

D_real = success × verified_work / cost × (1 - critical_risk) × (1 - coordination_overhead). If baselines win, replay fails, or the privacy boundary breaks, the claim is not promoted.

Boundary

This demo is browser-local. It does not collect data, move funds, connect wallets, call models, or authorize production actions.

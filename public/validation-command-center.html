Use the right validator.

AGI Nodes are excellent for deterministic public-safe checks. Humans handle judgment, public claims, legal/financial/security posture, publication, and high-impact outcomes. Hybrid is the best default for important work.

AGI Node

Fast mechanical precheck

Schema, route integrity, Evidence Docket completeness, replay readiness, no-data/no-wallet boundary, token-boundary wording.

Human

Judgment and accountability

Public claims, high-impact decisions, publication approval, procurement, legal/financial/security posture, external-action decisions.

Hybrid

Best default for important work

AGI Node prechecks the packet. A human reviewer decides whether to approve, reject, request changes, or escalate.

Council

Strategic and high novelty

Loop → RSI governance, Move-37 candidates, strategic dossiers, institutional promotion, state-capacity claims.

Hard rule

No authority without proof

A score is advisory. Gates are mandatory: proof, eval, scope, risk, replay, rollback, and challenge readiness.


## Objective

Open public review for `FRG‑001 — Frontier Model Release Evidence Docket Standard`.

## Why this matters

Frontier model release decisions need a repeatable proof surface: Mission Contracts, Claims Matrices, Evidence Dockets, access tiers, validator reports, rollback conditions, and human authority.

## Requested review

- Are the required docket fields sufficient?
- Are access tiers clear?
- Are validator seats appropriate?
- Is the public/private evidence split practical?
- What risks are missing?

## Acceptance criteria

- At least one external reviewer comment or public critique.
- Updated FRG‑001 reflecting concrete feedback.
- No unsupported claim expansion.

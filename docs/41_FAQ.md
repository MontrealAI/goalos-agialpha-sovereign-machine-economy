# FAQ

## What this is

FAQ is part of the GoalOS AGIALPHA Ascension documentation series. GoalOS turns one objective into a mission contract, proof debt, executable AGI Jobs, ProofBundle return paths, Evidence Docket 6.1, and a governed capability promotion decision.

## Why it matters

Institutions do not need more unsupported output. They need a clear path for deciding what was attempted, what proof exists, what remains blocked, what can be replayed, and what may safely influence future work.

## How to use it

Open the GoalOS V38 product console, type one objective, generate a fresh mission, review the AGI Jobs and ProofBundle templates, then export the dossier for human review.

## What it produces

Mission artifacts, proof artifacts, reviewer artifacts, schemas, documentation, GitHub Action assets, and a Chronicle hold record.

## What it does not claim

It does not claim achieved AGI, achieved ASI, empirical SOTA, legal certification, security certification, autonomous production authorization, automatic wallet execution, custody, live settlement, or guaranteed ROI.

## Boundary notes

Local mode is no-backend, no-wallet, no-user-funds, and intent-only for AGIJobManager. Live settlement belongs to a separate human-confirmed boundary.

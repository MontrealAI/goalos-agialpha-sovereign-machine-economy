# How to Review Site Institutional Command Center V14

1. Open `public/index.html`.
2. Press `/` and search for `RSI`, `loop`, `token`, `docket`, and `validator`.
3. Open `site-map.html` and confirm all pages are discoverable.
4. Open `demo-ecosystem-registry.html` and confirm routes have gates and next states.
5. Open `site-health.html` and inspect route-health reports.
6. Open `trust-boundary.html` and `token-boundary.html`.
7. Confirm no page asks for user data, funds, wallet connection, transaction, or production authority.

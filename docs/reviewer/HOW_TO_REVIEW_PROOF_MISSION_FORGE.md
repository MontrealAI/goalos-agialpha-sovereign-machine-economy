# How to Review the Proof Mission Forge

Reviewers should check:

1. Is the objective bounded?
2. Are sources public-safe?
3. Does the mission contain success and failure criteria?
4. Does the generated Claims Matrix distinguish supported, unsupported, and deferred claims?
5. Are baselines present?
6. Is replay possible?
7. Is the cost/risk ledger included?
8. Is the validator packet complete?
9. Does the issue draft contain no personal, customer, confidential, regulated, credential, wallet, payment, private-key, seed-phrase, or trade-secret data?

# How to Review the Proof-Backed Upgrade Rights Room

Reviewers should inspect:

1. Whether the page clearly explains what an upgrade right is.
2. Whether mandatory gates override the score.
3. Whether stress mode changes the decision state.
4. Whether downloads include the candidate, score, gates, and decision.
5. Whether the no-data / no-funds / no-wallet boundary remains visible.
6. Whether the page avoids claims of achieved AGI, ASI, legal approval, investment return, or production authorization.

A valid review may accept, reject, revise, or dissent.

# Revenue and Commercial Evidence Ledger

## Purpose

Commercial evidence should be handled carefully and privately by default. This ledger defines the public/private split.

## Private artifacts

- Signed order forms.
- Statements of work.
- Invoices.
- Payment records.
- Procurement emails.
- Renewal notices.

## Public-safe artifacts

- Aggregated counts.
- Non-sensitive ranges.
- Redacted acceptance notes.
- Evidence hashes.
- Anonymized conversion milestones.

## Review rule

Do not publish customer identity, revenue amount, contract detail, or legal term unless publication rights are clear.


## Claim discipline

This document is an operating standard. It does not claim financing, market valuation, production authorization, legal approval, user-fund authorization, revenue, enterprise adoption, or independent audit completion. Counted evidence must be dated, source-verifiable, and stored in the evidence room.

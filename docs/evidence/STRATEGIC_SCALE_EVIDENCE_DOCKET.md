# Strategic Scale Evidence Docket

## Purpose

The Strategic Scale Evidence Docket is the master packet used to evaluate whether the project has moved from institutional launch readiness into externally supported strategic scale.

## Docket sections

1. Product capability evidence.
2. Proof velocity evidence.
3. Customer discovery evidence.
4. Design partner evidence.
5. Pilot outcome evidence.
6. Usage and retention evidence.
7. Benchmark evidence.
8. Commercial evidence.
9. Ecosystem and partner evidence.
10. Independent validator evidence.
11. Fair-value-support evidence.
12. Governance and risk evidence.

## Required conclusion format

The conclusion must use one of these states:

- `infrastructure_ready_market_evidence_pending`
- `emerging_evidence_operator_attested`
- `validated_evidence_candidate`
- `strategic_scale_evidence_candidate`

Only the final state permits a strong market-scale public claim, and even then only with the attached evidence.


## Claim discipline

This document is an operating standard. It does not claim financing, market valuation, production authorization, legal approval, user-fund authorization, revenue, enterprise adoption, or independent audit completion. Counted evidence must be dated, source-verifiable, and stored in the evidence room.

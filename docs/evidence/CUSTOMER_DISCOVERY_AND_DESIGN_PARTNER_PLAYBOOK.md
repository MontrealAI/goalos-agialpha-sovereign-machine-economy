# Customer Discovery and Design Partner Playbook

## Purpose

This playbook converts stakeholder conversations into useful evidence while respecting privacy and consent.

## Discovery record

Each discovery record should include the date, stakeholder type, problem statement, current workflow, cost of the problem, urgency, buyer role, decision process, proof requested, and permission status.

## Design partner record

A design partner record should include the mission boundary, success criteria, timeline, expected artifacts, review cadence, confidentiality level, and conversion path.

## Evidence quality

The highest-quality signal is a dated, source-verifiable record tied to a bounded use case and a next-step commitment.


## Claim discipline

This document is an operating standard. It does not claim financing, market valuation, production authorization, legal approval, user-fund authorization, revenue, enterprise adoption, or independent audit completion. Counted evidence must be dated, source-verifiable, and stored in the evidence room.

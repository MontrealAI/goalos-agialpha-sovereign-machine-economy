# Governance for Strategic Claims

## Purpose

Strategic claims require a governance process because they affect trust, reputation, and stakeholder interpretation.

## Required review

- Claim owner.
- Evidence owner.
- Risk reviewer.
- Publication reviewer.
- Optional independent reviewer.

## Decision states

- `draft`
- `evidence_incomplete`
- `internally_supported`
- `externally_supported`
- `approved_for_publication`
- `rejected_or_rewritten`

## Rule

When evidence is incomplete, rewrite the claim rather than stretching the evidence.


## Claim discipline

This document is an operating standard. It does not claim financing, market valuation, production authorization, legal approval, user-fund authorization, revenue, enterprise adoption, or independent audit completion. Counted evidence must be dated, source-verifiable, and stored in the evidence room.

# Category Leadership Evidence Program

## Purpose

A category leadership claim requires more than a point solution. It requires proof of a new operating model, repeatable demand, independent review, and reusable capability.

## Program tracks

- Product proof.
- Use-case proof.
- Benchmark proof.
- Governance proof.
- Trust proof.
- Community proof.
- Commercial proof.
- Ecosystem proof.

## Publication cadence

Publish quarterly proof updates only after claim review. Each update should include what improved, what failed, what was validated, what remains pending, and what will be tested next.


## Claim discipline

This document is an operating standard. It does not claim financing, market valuation, production authorization, legal approval, user-fund authorization, revenue, enterprise adoption, or independent audit completion. Counted evidence must be dated, source-verifiable, and stored in the evidence room.

# Enterprise Traction Evidence Standard

## Purpose

Enterprise traction must be evidenced with structured records, not adjectives. This standard defines what counts.

## Counted evidence

- Signed pilot agreement or written pilot confirmation.
- Named use case with mission boundary.
- Success criteria agreed before execution.
- Evidence Docket after execution.
- Validator report or buyer acceptance record.
- Renewal, expansion, referral, procurement progression, or paid conversion record.

## Non-counted evidence

- Aspirational target accounts.
- Generic compliments without source.
- Unverified screenshots.
- Internal assumptions about willingness to pay.
- Pipeline entries without date, owner, and next step.


## Claim discipline

This document is an operating standard. It does not claim financing, market valuation, production authorization, legal approval, user-fund authorization, revenue, enterprise adoption, or independent audit completion. Counted evidence must be dated, source-verifiable, and stored in the evidence room.

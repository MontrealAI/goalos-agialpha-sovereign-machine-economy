# Pilot Outcome Docket Standard

## Purpose

Every serious pilot should end with a docket that captures what happened, what changed, and what evidence supports the result.

## Required fields

- Pilot objective.
- Counterparty type.
- Use case.
- Success criteria.
- Baseline.
- GoalOS workflow used.
- Evidence artifacts.
- Results.
- Risks and constraints.
- Validator decision.
- Follow-up status.
- Reuse opportunity.

## Outcome states

- `not_started`
- `in_progress`
- `completed_pending_review`
- `validated_success`
- `validated_mixed`
- `not_validated`


## Claim discipline

This document is an operating standard. It does not claim financing, market valuation, production authorization, legal approval, user-fund authorization, revenue, enterprise adoption, or independent audit completion. Counted evidence must be dated, source-verifiable, and stored in the evidence room.

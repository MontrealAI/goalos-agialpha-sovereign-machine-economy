# Independent Validation Protocol

## Purpose

Independent validation turns internal proof into institutional trust. The validator should be separate from the author of the work and should have a clear review rubric.

## Validator packet

- Mission Contract.
- Claims Matrix.
- ProofBundle.
- Evidence Docket.
- Risk Ledger.
- Cost Ledger.
- Replay instructions.
- Acceptance criteria.

## Validator outputs

- Decision.
- Confidence level.
- Evidence gaps.
- Reproduction notes.
- Conflicts or limitations.
- Publication recommendation.


## Claim discipline

This document is an operating standard. It does not claim financing, market valuation, production authorization, legal approval, user-fund authorization, revenue, enterprise adoption, or independent audit completion. Counted evidence must be dated, source-verifiable, and stored in the evidence room.

# Non-Technical User Guide — Multi-Agent Institution Lab

Open `multi-agent-institution.html` and click **Run proof flight**.

You will see GoalOS move through ten gates:

1. Mission
2. Roles
3. Tools
4. Evidence
5. Validation
6. Risk
7. Claim
8. Decision
9. Chronicle
10. Capability

The important idea is simple: more agents do not automatically mean better work. GoalOS only treats work as institution-ready when it has evidence, validation, risk controls, and human review.

Do not enter personal, customer, confidential, regulated, wallet, payment, private-key, seed-phrase, or trade-secret information. The demo is local only and does not send anything anywhere.

# Operator Experience Blueprint

The operator should feel in control at every step.

## Main operator screens

1. **Mission Intake** — what objective should be run?
2. **Mission Contract** — what counts as done?
3. **Proof Jobs** — what work packets were created?
4. **Trace Ledger** — what tools were used?
5. **Evidence Docket** — what proof exists?
6. **Validator Mesh** — who checked it?
7. **Decision State** — accept, revise, block, or release?
8. **Capability Registry** — what can be reused later?

## UX standard

Every screen must answer: “Can I trust this, and why?”
